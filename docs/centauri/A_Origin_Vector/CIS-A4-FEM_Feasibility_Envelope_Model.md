## 1. Purpose
Model the feasibility bounds of the concept using early simulations and analytical methods.

## 2. Inputs
- Hypothesis Sheet
- Scientific principles
- Mission constraints
- Environmental parameters

## 3. Outputs
- Feasibility envelope
- Boundary conditions
- Risk surfaces
- Sensitivity analysis

## 4. Method
1. Identify key variables
2. Build analytical or low-fidelity simulation models
3. Sweep parameter space
4. Identify feasibility regions

## 5. Mission Integration
- Determines whether the concept survives lunar environmental extremes
- Identifies required shielding, power, or thermal controls

## 6. TRL & SBIR Mapping
- TRL: 2
- SBIR: Pre-Phase I

## 7. Risks & Mitigations
- **Risk:** Model oversimplification
  **Mitigation:** Document assumptions
- **Risk:** Missing environmental factors
  **Mitigation:** Cross-check with mission environment data
