## 📍 1. Expansion Location: Shackleton Subsurface
The expansion will penetrate the regolith floor of Moonbase Alpha, extending into the stable thermal environment of the lunar crust.

### **Zonal Allocation (10,000 m²)**
1. **The Neural Core (4,000 m²):** Dedicated high-density server vaults for the AI training pipeline.
2. **Quantum Lab (1,500 m²):** Low-vibration, electromagnetically shielded environment for quantum compute prototypes.
3. **Logistics & Hydroponics (3,000 m²):** Expansion of life support and sustainable food production for a crew of 50+.
4. **The 10,000 Vault (1,500 m²):** Deep-storage archive for planetary history and network data.

## 🛠️ 2. Structural Specifications
- **Excavation Depth:** 15m - 50m below lunar datum.
- **Shielding:** Natural basaltic crust providing >3.0m of radiation protection.
- **Atmosphere:** Standard Earth (101.3 kPa) with independent zonal scrubbing.
- **Gravity:** 0.165g (standard lunar).

## ⚡ 3. Power & Cooling Integration
- **Heat Dissipation:** Geothermal coupling to the stable subsurface temperature (-20°C).
- **Power Coupling:** Direct bus connection to the 1MW backup nuclear reactor.
